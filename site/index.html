ABOUT ME


I was born at 10 am on May 1, 1990 in Potsdam, Germany.
My father was a military officer, now he works as a manager in a manufacturing holding, and my mother is an accountant.
When I was a child, we moved often.
Until I was 2 years old, we lived in Germany, then 2 years in Kursk, then 2 years in the Trans-Baikal Territory, and when I was 6 years old, we moved to Moscow.
In Moscow, I graduated from school, lyceum, and the Bauman Moscow State Technical University.
So I became an engineer specializing in the design and production of radio-electronic equipment.
I worked as a design engineer in various fields. I designed unmanned aircraft, special equipment and furniture, and I also tried to run my own business.
In 2020, I became interested in creating websites and games, which subsequently became a passion for me. That's how I became a software engineer.
All my life I have enjoyed sports and outdoor activities.
During my school years I was involved in biathlon and athletics.
When I was a student, I became actively interested in freediving, I became a Molchanovs freediving instructor.
And I also enjoy traveling, bicycling, running, swimming, SUP surfing, snowboarding, skiing, fishing, spearfishing, riding motorcycles, sailing, hiking, and much more.
